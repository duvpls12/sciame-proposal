Deliverable 02

Lifestyle Reels ×3

30–60 second aspirational Reels. Replaces the brand film with content that communicates who Sciame builds for — not through narration, but through feeling. Aspirational Palm Beach living tied directly to Sciame's finished work.

Waterfront Living

Golden hour on the water, dock or pool scene — evoking the effortless coastal lifestyle Sciame homes are built around.

Indoor/Outdoor Entertaining

Styled outdoor living space at dusk — showcasing how Sciame's design seamlessly blends interior luxury with the outdoors.

Morning Light Interior

Quiet luxury, coffee-table texture, natural light through floor-to-ceiling glass — the calm elegance of waking up in a Sciame home.

Designed to perform across social and the website without requiring a viewer to commit to a long-form video.
